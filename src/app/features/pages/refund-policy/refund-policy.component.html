Refund Policy

Insights Into - A Product of K-A Softech

3. Introduction

This Refund Policy sets out the terms under which you may request a refund for services provided by K-A Softech through Insights Into.

3.1 No Refund Policy

Non-Refundable Subscription:

All payments for Insights Into services are non-refundable. This includes any payment made for monthly subscriptions, even if you cancel the service mid-month.

Manual Renewal:

Subscriptions are manually renewed on a monthly basis. No automatic renewal will occur, and you are responsible for renewing the subscription each month.

3.2 Cancellation Process

How to Cancel:

You can cancel your subscription by simply choosing not to make the payment for the next month. No further charges will apply once your current subscription expires.

No Partial Refunds:

Since the service is billed monthly, no partial refunds will be issued for unused portions of the month.

3.3 Subscription Suspension

Payment Failure:

If your payment fails (due to issues like expired payment methods or insufficient funds), your subscription will be suspended until payment is completed. You will not be billed again until the payment is successfully processed.
